Design Philosophy 01

Twisted
Architecture

Understanding the desire to capture the immense beauty of nature in high-rise living, global architects have applied a smart twisted architecture solution for ALIZE Da Nang.

Every line is meticulously angled to break the rigid block structures, optimizing four-directional Panorama views. From the balcony, you can easily enjoy the brilliant sunrise on My Khe Beach, the peaceful poetic beauty of the Han River, or the vast majesty of the Son Tra Peninsula.
